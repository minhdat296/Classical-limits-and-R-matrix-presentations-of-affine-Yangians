    \title{Classical limits of affine Yangians}
    
    \author{Dat Minh Ha and Peggy Jankovic}
    \maketitle
    
    \begin{abstract}
        As a continuation of \cite{msc_thesis_gamma_extended_toroidal_lie_algebras}, we construct a topological Lie bialgebra structure on toroidal Lie algebras equipped with a bilinear form of degree $(0, -1)$. We then verify that, in the cases when the \say{underlying} affine Kac-Moody algebra is of a simply laced type, this coincides with the classical limits of the corresponding affine Yangians. Additionally, we make some preliminary connections with the theory of EALAs in the multi-loop realisation from \cite{allison_berman_faulkner_pianzola_multiloop_realisation_of_EALAs} via the theory of quantum toroidal algebra from \cite{laurie_toroidal_QUEs_coproducts_2}.
    \end{abstract}
